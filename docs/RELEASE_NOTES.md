# Lancet & Linen — Release Notes

## v1.3.1 — Readability & household

- The Hub measures its summary band before placing guidance and the work area, preventing the task/tip overlap on the market-stall screen.
- Courtship allows one substantial gesture per game day. Its social bonus is bounded, so marriage requires time, coin and an honourable name rather than repeated clicks.
- Household gifts are once per game day and require the spouse to be in the current city. Their affection gain is deliberately modest.
- A spouse’s portrait is tied to the chosen match. Household focus changes the household effect, never the person pictured.
- Release Notes are available from the start-menu’s **What’s new / Neu in dieser Version** button. The screen shows the current and preceding release in both supported languages.

## v1.3.0 — Cities & loading

- Augsburg linen and Nürnberg council agreements make later city choices durable and city-specific.
- Game content, translation runtime and Phaser are split into cacheable browser bundles.
- The iOS acceptance guide records required landscape checks for Safari and Chrome.

## Video policy

Short Grok-generated video is useful as optional, low-motion atmosphere only: a hearth, steam or incense loop with a static WebP fallback and reduced-particle support. It should not carry text, controls, rules, loading, or a required story beat. The playable web build keeps these loops small and local; longer cinematic video belongs in a trailer or store page.
